Ananas aus Caracas


Pedro's shop has delicious pineapples from Caracas. You want them, and  
dammit, you're going to get them.                                       

You happen to know that Pedro hides one pineapple on each floor of his  
shop. Find all twenty pineapples... before Pedro or his evil employees  
find you.                                                               


Difficulty:                                                             

   Casual                                                                  
   Normal                                                                  
   Hardcore
