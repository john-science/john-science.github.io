      Safe.

      You should think before typing your password into a strange page online. But this page is designed to protect you. This is a static webpage: no information you enter will be transmitted over the internet. The test is done 100% on YOUR computer. The information is yours, and it is only safe with you.

      Basic.

      This tool tests for all the usual problems with weak passwords. But the only "real" way to test if a password is easy to crack is to try and crack it. The procedure here would be to encrypt your password with a really simple algorithm (like MD5), download a modern password cracking program (like John the Ripper), and try and crack your password. That is a practical, real world test. However, I will not be providing that service free to the world.
